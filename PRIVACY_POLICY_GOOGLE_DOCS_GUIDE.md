# Privacy Policy on Google Docs - Quick Setup Guide

## Why Google Docs? ✅

- **Fastest** - Takes 5 minutes total
- **Safest** - Google reviewers accept it without question
- **Zero friction** - No website hosting, no SSL certificates, no technical setup
- **Professional** - Looks clean and official
- **Easy to update** - Edit anytime, changes reflect immediately

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Create a New Google Doc (2 minutes)

1. Go to **https://docs.google.com**
2. Click **"+ Blank"** to create a new document
3. Name it: **"Change In Youth - Privacy Policy"**

### Step 2: Copy & Paste the Privacy Policy (1 minute)

1. Open the **PRIVACY_POLICY.md** file from your project
2. Copy all the text (Ctrl+A, then Ctrl+C)
3. Paste it into your Google Doc (Ctrl+V)

**Or:** Copy the text below and paste it directly:

---

## Privacy Policy for Change In Youth

**Last Updated:** February 18, 2026

### Overview

Change In Youth ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

The App is currently in closed testing. This Privacy Policy applies to all users, including testers.

### What Information We Collect

**Camera Access**

The App requests permission to access your device's camera. The camera is used only for in-app functionality to support team management and communication features.

**Important:** We do not automatically collect, store, or transmit any images or videos from your camera. Images and videos are only captured when you actively choose to use camera features within the App.

**Account Information**

To use the App, you may need to create an account or log in. We collect:
- Your email address
- Your name
- Password (encrypted)
- Profile information you choose to provide

**Usage Information**

We may collect information about how you use the App, including:
- Features you access
- Actions you take
- Time and date of your activities
- Device type and operating system version

This information helps us improve the App and fix technical issues.

**Information You Provide**

When you use the App, you may voluntarily provide information such as:
- Messages and communications
- Task descriptions
- Financial records
- Team scheduling information

### How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide App functionality** – Enable you to use all features of the App
2. **Improve the App** – Understand how you use the App and make improvements
3. **Fix technical issues** – Diagnose and resolve problems
4. **Communicate with you** – Send important updates about the App
5. **Ensure security** – Protect your account and prevent misuse

### Camera Permission Details

**When We Access Your Camera**

Your camera is accessed only when you:
- Actively tap a button to use a camera feature
- Are actively recording or capturing within the App

**What Happens to Camera Data**

- **Not stored on our servers** – Images and videos remain on your device
- **Not transmitted automatically** – We only transmit data you explicitly choose to share
- **Under your control** – You decide what to do with any captured images or videos
- **Deleted when you want** – You can delete any captured content at any time

**Permission Revocation**

You can revoke camera permission at any time through your device settings:
- **Android:** Settings → Apps → Change In Youth → Permissions → Camera → Deny

If you revoke permission, you won't be able to use camera features in the App, but the App will continue to function normally for other features.

### Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including:
- Encrypting data in transit (using HTTPS)
- Encrypting passwords
- Restricting access to your information
- Regular security reviews

However, no security system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

### Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:
- Your account is active, or
- We need it to provide the App's services, or
- Required by law

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us (see below).

### Third Parties

**We Do NOT:**
- Sell your personal data
- Share your personal data with third parties for marketing
- Share your camera data with anyone
- Share your private messages or communications

**We MAY share information:**
- With service providers who help us operate the App (e.g., cloud storage providers)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or your safety
- With your explicit consent

### Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

### Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your information
- Export your information
- Opt-out of certain data collection

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above and, if necessary, by sending you a notification through the App.

Your continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

### Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

**Email:** privacy@changeinyouth.org.uk

**Organization:** Change In Youth CIC

We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.

### Additional Information for Google Play

- Minimal personal data collected

### Step 3: Format the Document (Optional, 1 minute)

To make it look more professional:

1. **Title:** Select the title, make it **bold** and increase font size to 18pt
2. **Headings:** Select section headings, make them **bold** and 14pt
3. **Font:** Change to "Calibri" or "Arial" for a professional look
4. **Spacing:** Set line spacing to 1.5 for readability

### Step 4: Share the Document (1 minute)

1. Click **"Share"** button (top-right corner)
2. Click **"Change to anyone with the link"**
3. Select **"Viewer"** (not Editor)
4. Click **"Copy link"**
5. Click **"Done"**

**Your link will look like:**
```
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ABC123XYZ/edit?usp=sharing
```

### Step 5: Add to Google Play Console (2 minutes)

1. Go to **https://play.google.com/console**
2. Select your app **"Change In Youth"**
3. Go to **"App content"** → **"App content"** (or similar section)
4. Find the **"Privacy policy"** field
5. Paste your Google Docs link
6. Click **"Save"**

## What Google Play Reviewers See

When Google reviewers check your app:

1. They click the privacy policy link
2. They see your Google Doc
3. They verify it covers the permissions you're using (camera)
4. They confirm you're not selling data
5. ✅ **Approved** - No issues

Google accepts Google Docs links without question. Thousands of apps use this method.

---

## Important Notes

✅ **DO:**
- Keep the link set to "Viewer" (not Editor)
- Update the policy if you add new permissions
- Keep the document accessible (don't delete it)
- Update the "Last Updated" date when you make changes

❌ **DO NOT:**
- Change the link to "Editor" (security risk)
- Delete the document after uploading
- Share the edit link (only share view link)
- Make the document private

## Troubleshooting

### Link Doesn't Work
- Make sure it's set to "Anyone with the link → Viewer"
- Try opening the link in a private/incognito window
- Copy the link again and test it

### Google Play Won't Accept Link
- Verify the link is publicly accessible
- Try a different browser
- Contact Google Play support

### Need to Update Privacy Policy
- Edit the Google Doc directly
- Changes appear immediately
- No need to update the link
- Update the "Last Updated" date
