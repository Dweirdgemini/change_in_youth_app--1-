# Google Play Store Publishing Guide - Change In Youth

## Prerequisites

- ✅ Google Play Developer Account ($25 one-time fee)
- ✅ App signing key (generated by Manus or manually)
- ✅ App icon and screenshots
- ✅ App description and privacy policy
- ✅ Version 1.0.3 ready to publish

## Step-by-Step Publishing Process

### Step 1: Prepare Your Google Play Developer Account

1. **Go to** [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console)
2. **Sign in** with your Google account
3. **Create a new app:**
   - Click "Create app"
   - App name: "Change In Youth"
   - Default language: English
   - App type: Application
   - Category: Business or Education
   - Click "Create app"

### Step 2: Fill in App Details

#### 2.1 App Information
1. **Go to** "App information" section
2. **Fill in:**
   - App name: "Change In Youth"
   - Short description: "Team management and financial platform for youth organizations"
   - Full description:
     ```
     Change In Youth is a comprehensive digital platform designed for youth 
     organizations delivering workshops and programs in schools.
     
     Features:
     - Team scheduling and rotas
     - Real-time financial tracking
     - Invoice management and approval workflow
     - Compliance documentation
     - Team communications and chat
     - Task management
     - Performance analytics
     
     Manage your entire operation from one mobile app.
     ```
   - Category: Business
   - Content rating: Select appropriate rating

#### 2.2 App Icon & Graphics
1. **Upload app icon** (512x512 px, PNG)
   - Use the icon from `assets/images/icon.png`
   - Must be high quality and clear at small sizes

2. **Upload screenshots** (minimum 2, maximum 8)
   - Dimensions: 1080x1920 px (9:16 aspect ratio)
   - Create screenshots showing:
     1. Home dashboard with stats
     2. Schedule/calendar view
     3. Financial management
     4. Task list
     5. Team chat
   - Add text overlays describing features

3. **Upload feature graphic** (1024x500 px, PNG/JPG)
   - Banner showing app name and key features
   - Use brand colors (deep blue background)

#### 2.3 Privacy Policy & Permissions
1. **Add privacy policy URL:**
   - Create privacy policy document
   - Host on your website (e.g., www.changeindelivery.org/privacy)
   - Link in Google Play Console

2. **Declare permissions:**
   - Location (for geolocation clock-in)
   - Camera (for invoice photo upload)
   - Contacts (for team member selection)
   - Calendar (for session scheduling)

### Step 3: Configure Release

#### 3.1 Create Release Track
1. **Go to** "Release" → "Production"
2. **Click** "Create new release"
3. **Upload APK or AAB:**
   - Option A: Use Manus-generated APK
     - Download from Manus platform
     - Upload here
   - Option B: Generate from Expo
     - Run: `eas build --platform android --auto-submit`
     - Download APK from EAS Build

#### 3.2 Set Version and Release Notes
1. **Version code:** 1 (auto-increments for each release)
2. **Version name:** 1.0.3
3. **Release notes:**
   ```
   Version 1.0.3 - Initial Launch
   
   Features:
   - Team scheduling and rotas
   - Financial management and invoicing
   - Task management
   - Team communications
   - Compliance documentation
   - Performance analytics
   
   Security improvements and bug fixes.
   ```

### Step 4: Content Rating Questionnaire

1. **Go to** "Content rating"
2. **Complete questionnaire:**
   - Violence: None
   - Sexual content: None
   - Profanity: None
   - Alcohol/tobacco: None
   - Gambling: None
   - Other: Declare any in-app communications or data collection

### Step 5: Target Audience

1. **Go to** "Target audience"
2. **Select:**
   - Age group: 13+ (or appropriate for your users)
   - Intended users: Business professionals, educators, youth workers

### Step 6: Pricing & Distribution

1. **Go to** "Pricing & distribution"
2. **Set pricing:**
   - Free (if offering free tier)
   - Or select paid option with price
3. **Select countries** where app is available
4. **Declare:**
   - Ads: None (or if included)
   - In-app purchases: Yes/No
   - Restricted content: None

### Step 7: Review & Submit

1. **Review all sections** for completeness
2. **Check for warnings** (fix any red flags)
3. **Click "Review release"**
4. **Click "Publish to Production"**

### Step 8: Verification & Approval

**Timeline:** 2-24 hours typically

**Google will check:**
- ✅ App functionality
- ✅ Privacy policy accuracy
- ✅ Permissions justified
- ✅ No policy violations
- ✅ Content rating accuracy

**If rejected:**
- Review rejection reason
- Fix issues
- Resubmit for review

## Troubleshooting

### App Rejected for Policy Violations
**Common issues:**
- Privacy policy not clear about data collection
- Permissions not justified in app
- Misleading description or screenshots
- Unsafe content in chat features

**Solution:**
- Review rejection email carefully
- Fix the specific issue
- Resubmit with explanation

### Crash Reports After Launch
**Immediate action:**
1. Check crash reports in Google Play Console
2. Identify affected devices/Android versions
3. Release hotfix version (1.0.4)
4. Test thoroughly before resubmit

### Low Ratings
**Common feedback:**
- App crashes
- Slow performance
- Confusing interface
- Missing features

**Response:**
- Address top complaints in next version
- Respond to reviews professionally
- Encourage users to email support instead of leaving 1-star reviews
