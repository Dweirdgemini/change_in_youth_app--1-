## Presentation Tips

### Delivery
- **Pacing:** 3-4 minutes per slide (total 70-85 minutes with Q&A)
- **Tone:** Professional but approachable — you're solving real problems
- **Engagement:** Ask questions, involve the audience, tell stories

### Visual Design
- Use consistent branding colors (deep blue, green, amber)
- Include real app screenshots for authenticity
- Use data visualizations for metrics and comparisons
- Keep text minimal — let visuals tell the story

### Customization
- Add your organization's logo and branding
- Include case studies or testimonials if available
- Adjust pricing and timeline based on specific client needs
- Tailor use cases to match the client's industry and challenges
