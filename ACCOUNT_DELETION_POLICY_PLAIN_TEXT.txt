ACCOUNT DELETION POLICY
Change In Youth CIC
Effective Date: February 24, 2026

================================================================================
HOW TO REQUEST ACCOUNT DELETION
================================================================================

Users can request deletion of their account by contacting our support team at:

Email: support@changeinyouth.org.uk
Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request"

Required Information in Request:
- Your registered email address
- Your full name (as registered in the app)
- Brief reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your account deletion request within 30 days of receiving your email.

================================================================================
WHAT GETS DELETED
================================================================================

When you request account deletion, the following data associated with your account 
will be permanently deleted:

- Your account profile (name, email, password)
- Your personal settings and preferences
- Any user-generated content you created in the app
- Your account activity history

================================================================================
WHAT MAY BE RETAINED
================================================================================

For legal, security, and operational reasons, the following data may be retained 
after account deletion:

AGGREGATED ANALYTICS DATA
Non-personally identifiable usage statistics (e.g., feature usage counts, app crash 
reports) that cannot be traced back to you.

FINANCIAL RECORDS
Transaction records and invoices required for accounting, tax compliance, and legal 
obligations (retained for 7 years per UK tax law).

SUPPORT COMMUNICATIONS
Copies of support tickets and communications with our team (retained for dispute 
resolution).

LEGAL HOLDS
Data retained due to legal proceedings, court orders, or regulatory requirements.

BACKUP DATA
Your data may remain in our backup systems for up to 90 days after deletion for 
disaster recovery purposes.

================================================================================
TIMELINE
================================================================================

Request Submission
Send email to support@changeinyouth.org.uk

Confirmation
We will confirm receipt of your request within 2 business days

Processing
Account deletion will be completed within 30 days

Completion Confirmation
You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

================================================================================
YOUR RIGHTS
================================================================================

Under UK Data Protection Law (UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018), you have the 
right to:

- Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Request a copy of your personal data in a portable format
- Request correction of inaccurate personal data
- Object to processing of your personal data for certain purposes

For any data privacy inquiries, contact: privacy@changeinyouth.org.uk
