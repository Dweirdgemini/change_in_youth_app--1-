COMMUNICATION & COLLABORATION

ANALYTICS & INSIGHTS

insights to drive organizational growth.

Perfect for:

• Social enterprises and NGOs
• Small to medium-sized teams

• Teams requiring financial transparency

Change In Youth empowers organizations to work smarter, stay organized, and 
achieve their mission with confidence.

## How to Add to Google Play Console

1. Go to https://play.google.com/console
2. Select your app "Change In Youth"
3. Click **"Store listing"** in left sidebar
4. Fill in each section:
   - **App name:** Change In Youth
   - **Short description:** [Use short description above]
   - **Full description:** [Use full description above]
   - **Screenshots:** Upload 5-8 screenshots
   - **Feature graphic:** Upload 1024x500px image
   - **Privacy policy:** Paste Google Docs link
5. Click **"Save"** after each section
6. Review all sections
7. Click **"Review and publish"** when ready
