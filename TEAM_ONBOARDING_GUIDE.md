# Community Work Space App - Team Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the Change In Youth CIC Team Management & Financial Platform! This guide will help you get started with the app.

---

## 📱 **Getting Started**

### **Option 1: Mobile App (Recommended)**

#### **Step 1: Install Expo Go**
1. Open the **App Store** (iOS) or **Google Play Store** (Android)
2. Search for **"Expo Go"**
3. Download and install the app
4. Open Expo Go after installation

#### **Step 2: Access the Community Work Space App**
1. Open the link sent to you by your administrator
2. Your phone will ask "Open in Expo Go?" - tap **Yes**
3. The Community Work Space app will load in Expo Go
4. Wait for the app to fully load (may take 30-60 seconds on first launch)

### **Option 2: Web Browser**
1. Open the link sent to you in your mobile browser (Chrome, Safari, etc.)
2. The app will load as a web application
3. For best experience, add it to your home screen:
   - **iOS**: Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
   - **Android**: Tap the menu (⋮) → "Add to Home screen"

---

## 🔐 **Logging In**

### **Step 1: Open the App**
- You'll see the **Dashboard** or **Login Screen**

### **Step 2: Sign In with Google**
1. Tap the **"Sign in with Google"** button
2. A browser window will open
3. Select your **Google account** (use your work/organization email)
4. Review the permissions and tap **"Allow"** or **"Continue"**
5. You'll be redirected back to the app automatically

### **Step 3: First-Time Setup**
- After logging in for the first time, you may need to wait for an administrator to assign you a role
- Your role determines what features you can access:
  - **Admin**: Full access to all features
  - **Finance**: Access to budget and financial management
  - **Team Member**: Access to projects, tasks, and team chat
  - **Social Media Manager**: Access to media approval workflows

## 💬 **Getting Help**

### **Common Issues**

**Problem: "Can't log in with Google"**
- Make sure you're using your work/organization Google account
- Check your internet connection
- Try closing and reopening the app
- Contact your administrator if the issue persists

**Problem: "I don't have access to certain features"**
- Your role may not have permission for that feature
- Contact your administrator to request access or role change

**Problem: "App is slow or not loading"**
- Check your internet connection
- Close and reopen the app
- If using Expo Go, try reloading: shake your phone → tap "Reload"

**Problem: "I see 'Quick Admin Login' button"**
- This is a testing feature - ignore it and use the Google Sign In button instead
