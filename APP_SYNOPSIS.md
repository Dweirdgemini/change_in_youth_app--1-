# Change In Youth CIC - Mobile App Synopsis

## Executive Summary

**Change In Youth** is a comprehensive digital platform designed for youth organizations delivering workshops and programs in schools. The app transforms how teams manage operations by consolidating team coordination, financial tracking, compliance documentation, and real-time communications into a single, intuitive mobile interface.

**Target Users:** Program managers, facilitators, finance staff, and administrative teams at youth-focused organizations.

**Core Value Proposition:** Eliminate spreadsheets, paper forms, and fragmented tools. Replace them with a unified digital office that automates workflows, ensures compliance, and provides real-time visibility into operations and finances.

---

## Key Features

### 1. **Team Management & Scheduling**
- **Rota Management:** Calendar-based session scheduling with facilitator assignment
- **Availability Tracking:** Real-time team availability to prevent conflicts
- **Clock-In/Out:** Geolocation-based time tracking at session venues
- **Team Analytics:** Performance metrics for facilitator pairings and session outcomes

### 2. **Financial Management**
- **Budget Tracking:** Real-time budget overview per project with visual progress indicators
- **Invoice Management:** Upload invoices (photo/PDF) with AI-powered auto-categorization
- **Approval Workflow:** Finance team approval process with automatic budget deduction
- **Payment Tracking:** Expected payments list with automated reminders to facilitators
- **Financial Reporting:** Budget line breakdown, spending analytics, payment history

### 3. **Task & Project Management**
- **Task Creation & Assignment:** Create tasks linked to projects and sessions
- **Progress Tracking:** Completion checklists with automated payment eligibility verification
- **Filtering & Search:** Filter by assignee, project, status, and priority
- **Automated Workflows:** Tasks trigger payment eligibility checks and notifications

### 4. **Compliance & Documentation**
- **Document Library:** Centralized storage for consent forms, evaluations, and registers
- **Session Checklists:** Automated reminders for missing documentation
- **Digital Signatures:** Collect signatures on consent forms and agreements
- **Form Templates:** Pre-built templates for common compliance documents
- **Audit Trail:** Complete history of all documentation activities

### 5. **Communications & Collaboration**
- **Team Chat:** Project-specific channels and general team messaging
- **Safe Student Forum:** Moderated discussion space for youth participants
- **Direct Messaging:** One-on-one communication between team members
- **File Sharing:** Share documents, images, and reports within channels
- **Announcements:** Broadcast important updates to the entire team

### 6. **Surveys & Feedback**
- **Survey Builder:** Create custom surveys with multiple question types
- **Response Tracking:** Real-time response counts and completion status
- **Data Export:** Export survey results for analysis and reporting
- **Feedback Collection:** Gather participant and facilitator feedback

### 7. **Dashboard & Reporting**
- **Home Dashboard:** Quick stats on upcoming sessions, pending invoices, budget status
- **Real-Time Activity Feed:** Recent payments, completed tasks, new messages
- **Quick Actions:** One-tap access to clock in, submit invoices, create tasks
- **Performance Metrics:** Team productivity, session attendance, budget utilization

## Technical Highlights

### Mobile-First Design
- **Native iOS/Android Feel:** Built with React Native and Expo for authentic mobile experience
- **Offline-Capable:** Core functionality works without internet connection
- **One-Handed Usage:** Optimized for mobile portrait orientation (9:16)
- **Dark Mode Support:** Professional appearance in all lighting conditions

### Security & Compliance
- **OAuth Authentication:** Secure login with Manus platform integration
- **Role-Based Access Control:** Admin, Finance, Facilitator, and Participant roles
- **Data Encryption:** Secure storage of sensitive financial and personal data
- **Audit Logging:** Complete activity history for compliance and accountability
- **GDPR Compliant:** Built with data privacy and protection in mind

### Scalability & Performance
- **Cloud-Ready:** Deployed on scalable cloud infrastructure
- **Real-Time Sync:** Instant updates across all team members
- **Database-Backed:** MySQL database for reliable data persistence
- **API-First Architecture:** RESTful and tRPC APIs for flexible integrations

## Use Cases

### Scenario 1: Weekly Session Planning
**Manager:** Opens app, views team availability, schedules 5 sessions for the week, assigns facilitators based on performance metrics. System automatically sends notifications to assigned facilitators.

### Scenario 2: Invoice Submission & Approval
**Facilitator:** Takes photo of receipt, uploads to app. AI auto-categorizes expense. Finance team receives notification, reviews, approves. Budget automatically deducted. Facilitator receives payment confirmation.

### Scenario 3: Compliance Documentation
**Program Lead:** Creates session documentation checklist. Facilitators upload attendance register, consent forms, and evaluations. System flags missing documents. Manager reviews all documents in one place before session sign-off.

### Scenario 4: Team Communication**
**Team:** Uses project-specific chat to discuss session logistics, share updates, and resolve issues in real-time. Student forum allows safe peer-to-peer discussion moderated by staff.
