# Account Deletion Policy

**Effective Date:** February 24, 2026

## Overview

Change In Youth is committed to respecting user privacy and data rights. This policy explains how users can request deletion of their account and personal data.

## How to Delete Your Account

### In-App Deletion (Recommended)

1. Open the Change In Youth app
2. Navigate to **More** → **Settings**
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Read the warning carefully
5. Enter your email address to confirm
6. Tap **Delete permanently**
7. Your account will be immediately deleted

### Email-Based Deletion

If you prefer to request deletion by email, contact:

**Email:** support@changeinyouth.org.uk

**Include in your email:**
- Your full name
- Email address associated with your account
- Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion via email.

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following personal data is permanently removed:

- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **Profile picture**
- **Push notification tokens**
- **Authentication tokens**

## What May Be Retained

The following data may be retained for legitimate business purposes:

- **Audit logs** — Anonymized records of the deletion action (timestamp, request ID only)
- **Financial records** — Invoices, payslips, and payment history (required for UK tax compliance)
- **Backup copies** — May exist for up to 30 days in automated backups before permanent deletion
- **Anonymized analytics** — Aggregate usage data that cannot identify you

## Data Transmission Security

All data transmission between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Your authentication tokens are stored securely and are invalidated immediately upon account deletion.

## Deletion Timeline

- **Immediate:** Personal data (name, email, profile image) is anonymized
- **Immediate:** Authentication tokens are invalidated
- **Within 30 days:** Complete deletion from all backups

## Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

- Request deletion of your personal data
- Receive confirmation of deletion
- Request information about what data we hold
- Request a copy of your data before deletion

## Questions or Concerns

If you have questions about account deletion or data privacy, please contact:

**Email:** support@changeinyouth.org.uk

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
