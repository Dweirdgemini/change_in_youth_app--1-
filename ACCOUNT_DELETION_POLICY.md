# Account Deletion Policy

**Effective Date:** February 24, 2026

## How to Request Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account by contacting our support team at:

**Email:** support@changeinyouth.org.uk

**Subject Line:** "Account Deletion Request"

**Required Information in Request:**
- Your registered email address
- Your full name (as registered in the app)
- Brief reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your account deletion request within **30 days** of receiving your email.

---

## What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted:

- Your account profile (name, email, password)
- Your personal settings and preferences
- Any user-generated content you created in the app
- Your account activity history

---

## What May Be Retained

For legal, security, and operational reasons, the following data may be retained after account deletion:

- **Aggregated Analytics Data:** Non-personally identifiable usage statistics (e.g., feature usage counts, app crash reports) that cannot be traced back to you
- **Financial Records:** Transaction records and invoices required for accounting, tax compliance, and legal obligations (retained for 7 years per UK tax law)
- **Support Communications:** Copies of support tickets and communications with our team (retained for dispute resolution)
- **Legal Holds:** Data retained due to legal proceedings, court orders, or regulatory requirements
- **Backup Data:** Your data may remain in our backup systems for up to 90 days after deletion for disaster recovery purposes

---

## Timeline

- **Request Submission:** Send email to support@changeinyouth.org.uk
- **Confirmation:** We will confirm receipt of your request within 2 business days
- **Processing:** Account deletion will be completed within 30 days
- **Completion Confirmation:** You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

---

## Your Rights

Under UK Data Protection Law (UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018), you have the right to:

- **Request deletion** of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Request a copy** of your personal data in a portable format
- **Request correction** of inaccurate personal data
- **Object to processing** of your personal data for certain purposes

For any data privacy inquiries, contact: **privacy@changeinyouth.org.uk**
